About our company


Eversun Marine Trade was formed in 1999 with the vision to revitalize the bulk cargo handling in Visakhapatnam Port. With the onset of operating FC. Bulk Express & Ravi B, two floating crane vessels were put in place in the general cargo berth for lighter-age & discharge operations, which brought on the handling panamax vessels for the first time in Visakhapatnam Port.

Corporate Social Responsibility


As Part of CSR initiative, EMTPL donates to Akshaya Patra Visakhapatnam; The Organisation strives to fight issues like hunger and malnutrition in India. Akshaya Patra aims not only to fight hunger but focuses on enrolling students to school.
We support Hrudaya Foundation Hyderabad, a non-profit organization, established primarily to raise funds that will be used to provide treatment to poor children affected with congenital heart diseases.

Table India is a national level initiative known as " freedom through education". We work with impoverished & underfunded schools by providing development & augmentation.

Round Table India has around 200 tables located in 76 cities & towns. Vizag Round Table 77 believes in bringing change through excellence. VRT77 works locally & where we meet regularly, plans & organise meetings for community development.
